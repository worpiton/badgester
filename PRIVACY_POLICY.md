### Privacy Policy

**Introduction**

This Privacy Policy explains how Badgester collects, uses, and protects your information. By using our bot, you agree to the terms of this policy.

**1. Information We Collect**

- We collect the following data: Roblox user IDs, usernames, display names, and cursors.
- We also store temporary logs of command usage, including Discord user ID and command links.

**2. How We Use Information**

- The collected data is used to provide and improve our services.
- Temporary logs are used for monitoring and debugging purposes and are deleted after a reboot.

**3. Information Disclosure**

- We do not disclose your information to third parties except as required by law.

**4. Data Security**

- We take all necessary measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

**5. User Rights**

- You have the right to request the deletion of your data from our database. If you request deletion, you will be blocked from using the bot.

**6. Cookies and Similar Technologies**

- We do not use cookies or similar technologies.

**7. Changes to Privacy Policy**

- We may make changes to this Privacy Policy. Changes take effect upon publication.

**8. Contact Information**

- For any questions or complaints, please contact us at worpiton@enchoral.me

**9. Additional Provisions for Users in Europe and America**

- Users in Europe and America must comply with the relevant data protection regulations, including GDPR for European users.
- Users must be at least 13 years old to use Badgester. If you are between 13 and 17 years old, you must have permission from a parent or guardian.
